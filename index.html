Highway 4 Boulders

This is a collection of first ascents by Ezra Salomon off Spicer Reservior Road.

Area Overview:

The boulders:

Napoleon Complex V7

Small erratic ontop of the ridge. Board problem, staight forward pulling on crimps!

King Knave V6

Start at the lowest part of the prow, speezing either side and climb, topping out on the left.

Flying Dutchman V8

Sit start matching the small block. Climb up and left.

Hive Mind V7

Sit start in the pit using a left hand side pull and a right hand crimp. Trend right and up to the large rail.

Timber Terror V7

Start matched on the jagged edge at chest height, climb up and right, big move to the lip. Bring pads and a spotter.

Between the Pines V3

Start on a large jug at head height and climb left up the crack and then up. Tall but good landing.
